AI PING PONG

Instructions

1. First keep your laptop screen straight.

2. Move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
3. Move your right wrist in ascending and descending manner and a red dot should appear on your wrist.
4. Now press the play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
5. The red paddel is your paddel, that will move as per your right wrist.
